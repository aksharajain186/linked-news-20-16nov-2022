Shraddha murder: Aaftab sent to 5-day police custody, court allows narco test

The Delhi Police had sought further remand of the accused saying that the cops have to visit Parvati Valley in Himachal Pradesh to dig up more evidence.

A Delhi court on Thursday sent Aaftab Amin Poonawala, who allegedly killed his live-in partner Shraddha Walkar, to five-day police custody and allowed a narco-analysis test on him.

Poonawala was produced before the Saket Court through video conference at 4pm. The Delhi Police had filed an application requesting the court to produce Poonawala virtually due to his security concerns.

Metropolitan magistrate Aviral Shukla said according to the application submitted by police, the accused faced threats from miscreants and religious groups.
