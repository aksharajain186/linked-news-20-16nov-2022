h1>Meta India gets new head: All you need to know about Sandhya Devanathan

According to a Meta statement, Devanathan will assume her role as Meta India's Vice President on January 1. She succeeds Ajit Mohan, who left company earlier this month.

On Thursday, Meta announced it has appointed Sandhya Devanathan as the Vice President of its India unit, a role in which she will head the company's business in the country.

In a statement, Meta said Devanathan's stint as the Vice President of Meta India will begin on January 1, and she will relocate to the country to head the organisation (according to her LinkedIn profile, she is currently based in Singapore). Devanathan succeeds Ajit Mohan, who resigned on November 3; Manish Chopra, Meta India's director and head of partnerships, has taken over Mohan's position on an interim basis.

(1.) She joined Meta (then known as Facebook) in January 2016, as Group Director, SEA - Ecommerce, Travel, and FinServ, in Singapore. In August, she became the tech giant's Managing Director for Singapore, and Business Head for Vietnam.
